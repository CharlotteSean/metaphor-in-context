Notes for each repo:

corpora: contains raw datasets published online by researchers. can ignore.
data: contains formatted version of each corpus. Details see notes in data repo.
elmo: elmo vectors for MOH-X, TroFi and VUA with train/dev/test division
glove: 300D glove vectors 

baseline: lexical baseline for MOH-X (10-fold cross-validation), TroFi and VUA.
word: feedforward net
context: LSTM for verb
sequence: LSTM for sequence

predictions: predictions on validation datsaset
savedModelsLSTM: optimal LSTM models
models: temp repo for saved models during training. can ignore.
